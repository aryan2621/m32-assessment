# Invoice & Expense Copilot

An AI-powered invoice and expense management platform that automates invoice processing, tracks expenses, and provides intelligent insights through natural language interaction.

## About

Invoice & Expense Copilot is a comprehensive financial management solution designed to help businesses and individuals streamline their invoice and expense tracking. The platform uses advanced AI technology to automatically extract information from invoices, eliminating manual data entry and reducing errors.

## What It Does

### Automated Invoice Processing

Simply upload your invoices (PDF or images) and let AI do the work. The system automatically extracts:

- Vendor information (name, email, phone number)
- Invoice numbers and dates
- Line items with quantities and prices
- Tax amounts and totals
- Payment due dates

No more manual data entry or spreadsheet management.

### Intelligent Expense Tracking

Keep track of all your expenses in one place:

- Automatic expense creation from processed invoices
- Manual expense entry with categorization
- Search and filter expenses by date, vendor, category, or amount
- Vendor tracking and management

### AI Chat Assistant

Ask questions about your financial data in plain English:

- "Show me this month's expenses"
- "Which vendor charged the most?"
- "What are my top expense categories?"
- "Analyze my spending trends"

The AI assistant understands your questions and provides instant insights.

### Analytics & Insights

Get a clear picture of your finances:

- Interactive charts showing spending trends
- Category-based breakdowns
- Top vendor analysis
- Dashboard with key financial metrics
- Time-based filtering and comparisons

### Google Drive Integration

Connect your Google Drive to automatically sync invoices:

- Invoices in your Drive are automatically imported
- Seamless integration with existing workflows
- No need to manually upload files

## Key Features

- **Smart Document Processing**: Upload PDF or image invoices (JPEG, PNG, WebP) and get structured data automatically
- **Natural Language Queries**: Ask questions about your finances in everyday language
- **Real-time Processing**: See invoice processing progress in real-time
- **Secure Storage**: Your financial data is encrypted and stored securely
- **Multi-format Support**: Works with PDF and image invoice formats
- **Categorization**: Automatically categorize invoices and expenses
- **Status Tracking**: Track invoice payment status (pending, paid, overdue)
- **Visual Analytics**: Beautiful charts and graphs to visualize your financial data
- **Search & Filter**: Quickly find specific invoices or expenses
- **Export Capabilities**: Export your data for external analysis

## How It Works

1. **Upload Invoices**: Drag and drop or select invoice files (PDF or images)
2. **AI Processing**: The system automatically extracts all relevant information
3. **Review & Edit**: Review the extracted data and make any necessary corrections
4. **Track Expenses**: Expenses are automatically created from processed invoices
5. **Get Insights**: Use the chat assistant or analytics dashboard to understand your finances
6. **Stay Organized**: All your invoices and expenses are organized and searchable
